## What it is

The Commodore 64's *Project: Space Station* was a brilliant little management game that never got the sequel it deserved. This is that sequel — only now you're building a base on Mars instead of assembling one in Earth orbit. 12-bit aesthetic, phase-based gameplay, and the kind of quiet tension that comes from planning a mission you can't take back.

## Phases

- **Plan** — mission goals, cargo, crew
- **Launch** — the one-shot moment that decides everything
- **Transit / arrival** — the slow part, where things go wrong
- **Base operations** — day-to-day life on Mars
- **R&D** — unlock what you need for the *next* mission
- **Progression** — longer, harder, farther

## Status

Playable desktop build running on Windows and Raspberry Pi. Active work is on the R&D loop and random events.
