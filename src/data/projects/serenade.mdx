## What it is

You're a troubadour performing classical pieces beneath a lady's balcony. Hit the right notes at the right time and she throws flowers. Miss too many and she throws a bottle. It's a tiny, charming rhythm game from 1986 that almost nobody remembers, and I remade it in the browser because I felt like it.

## How it plays

- Seven classical pieces, progressively harder (Twinkle Twinkle → full Vivaldi)
- Notes scroll down to a hit zone at the bottom
- `A S D F G H J` maps to `C D E F G A B`
- Three mistakes and the lady leaves

## Why

Nostalgia, mostly. But also: the original is a tidy little example of how much personality you can squeeze into a few hundred lines of code and a handful of sprites. Remaking it was a good reminder that scope and charm are not the same thing.
