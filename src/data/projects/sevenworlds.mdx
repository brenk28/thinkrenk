## What it is

You captain a small exploration team tasked with scouting and establishing outposts across seven procedurally arranged worlds. Pitched somewhere between *Seven Cities of Gold* and *Star Control*, with the same 12-bit aesthetic I'm chasing in Egyptology — more generous than C64, more pixel-honest than modern.

## Systems

- **Stellar cartography** — discover, name, and map systems you've visited
- **Away missions** — land, explore surfaces, recover artifacts, meet natives
- **Crew & resources** — food, fuel, morale, the things that keep a ship moving
- **Discovery journal** — the game's memory of what you've found

## Status

The project started as an Electron/React prototype and is being ported to Godot 4.3 for visual parity with the rest of the retro-game lineup. There's a detailed parity plan tracked in the repo.
