## What it is

Type a premise, get a short story with per-scene illustrations. The app chains a text model for prose with an image model for scene art and stitches them into a readable page. Built to explore the orchestration patterns more than the product — it's a great sandbox for prompt caching, tool use, and cost shaping.

## What I'm learning here

- How to structure multi-step generation so partial results stream to the user
- Where to spend model calls and where to cheat with templates
- How much of "good output" comes from the prompt vs. the pipeline around it
